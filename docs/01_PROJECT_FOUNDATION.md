# WashGo – Project Foundation

## Project Overview

**WashGo** is a modern on-demand laundry pickup and delivery platform designed for Cambodia, beginning with Siem Reap. The platform connects customers, laundry shops, and delivery riders through a single ecosystem that simplifies the entire laundry process.

Customers can schedule laundry pickups from their homes, dormitories, hotels, or workplaces. A rider collects the laundry, delivers it to the selected laundry shop, and returns the cleaned clothes after completion. The platform provides real-time order tracking, digital payments, notifications, and transparent pricing.

WashGo aims to become the "Food Delivery App for Laundry Services."

---

# Vision

To become Cambodia's leading digital laundry platform by making laundry services fast, convenient, reliable, and accessible for everyone.

---

# Mission

- Digitize traditional laundry businesses.
- Save customers time through pickup and delivery.
- Create additional income opportunities for delivery riders.
- Provide laundry shops with more customers through digital technology.
- Deliver a premium customer experience with transparency and reliability.

---

# Problem Statement

Traditional laundry services in Cambodia commonly experience several issues:

- Customers must travel to and from the laundry shop.
- There is little transparency regarding order status.
- Communication between customers and laundry shops is inefficient.
- Delivery services are often unavailable or inconsistent.
- Payments are usually cash-only.
- Customers cannot easily compare nearby laundry shops.

These problems reduce convenience and limit business growth.

---

# Proposed Solution

WashGo provides a complete digital platform consisting of:

- Customer Mobile Application
- Rider Mobile Application
- Laundry Shop Dashboard
- Admin Web Dashboard

The system manages the complete laundry workflow from pickup request to final delivery.

# Core Business Workflow

1. Customer creates an account.
2. Customer selects a laundry shop.
3. Customer schedules a pickup.
4. System assigns a rider.
5. Rider collects laundry.
6. Rider delivers laundry to the shop.
7. Laundry shop processes the order.
8. Shop marks the order as ready.
9. Rider collects cleaned laundry.
10. Rider delivers laundry to the customer.
11. Customer confirms successful delivery.
12. Payment is completed and the order is closed.

# Project Philosophy

WashGo is designed around one simple principle:

> "Laundry should be as easy to order as food delivery."

Every design decision should prioritize simplicity, speed, reliability, and an outstanding user experience for customers, riders, laundry shops, and administrators.
